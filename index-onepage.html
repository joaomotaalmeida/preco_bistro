Our Story

Spain meets Slovakia

We are happy that we’ve won the 1st place within the Spanish week 2018 and 2019 – “Ruta de la tapa” competition, among other tapas and wines experts from Bratislava. At “Prečo-Bistro”, we’re creating unique degustation fusion of Slovak and Spanish cuisine, bringing you typical tapas variations, combined with delicious Iberian wines.

Spain is well known for its diverse colorful tastes and delicious food and wine, typical for each Spanish region and Iberian Peninsula.

After more than 10 years of chef experience, traveling and discovering Spain and France, local culinary specialties and different wine flavors, my heart and love to my wife, brought me to the heart of Europe, Slovakia.

And why is it “Prečo-Bistro”? It’s the passion for gastronomy, assorted by an honest interest in customer to offer special enjoyable service, full of wine and tapas in a cozy place with an affordable price.

Just book a table and we can chat not only about the typical Spanish food experience and wine types. Feel free to make a reservation or contact us directly.
